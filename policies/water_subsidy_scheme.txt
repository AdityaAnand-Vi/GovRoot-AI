UP Jal Jeevan Mission - Rural Water Subsidy Scheme 2024
Eligibility:
1. Resident of Sector Alpha, Beta, or Gamma.
2. Annual household income < ₹1,00,000.
3. Land holding < 2 hectares.
4. No previous water subsidies in the last 3 fiscal years.
Benefits: 50% subsidy on connection costs (max ₹25,000) via DBT to Jan Dhan accounts.
